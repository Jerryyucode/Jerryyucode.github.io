Who we are

At StudyWise, we are a team of educators, mentors, and advisors who share a single mission: empowering students to reach their full academic potential.

Our instructors and counselors are graduates of renowned private high schools and leading universities — including Stanford, MIT, UCLA, UC Berkeley, Cornell, and the University of Chicago — as well as top graduate programs. We understand the challenges of today’s academic landscape because we have navigated them ourselves and excelled. Now, we’re dedicated to guiding the next generation of students through every step of their journey.

With deep expertise across subjects and a passion for teaching, our team offers personalized support in two main areas:

  • Tutoring & Test Prep: Subject tutoring, competition preparation, AP/IB coursework, and standardized test preparation tailored to each student’s learning style.
  • Admissions Consulting: Comprehensive guidance for applications to private schools, colleges, and graduate programs, including strategy, essay coaching, and interview preparation.

We believe the right guidance can change a student’s life. That’s why our approach is supportive, results-driven, and customized for every learner.

At StudyWise, we’re here to help you rise and shine.
